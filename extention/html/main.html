Welcome!

Aww yeah, you successfully install this amazing chrome extension!

All data will synchronize with cloud service. Without Internet connection, you can read cached version of your tasks, but you cannot interact with them.

First of all, generate your unique identifier. To do it, click on extension icon in the upper right corner of browser (or somewhere else, if you don't use Google Chrome). Click "Generate New" and you will see your new id. DON'T forget it! Without it, you can't get access to your data.

After getting id, open new tab (or reload this) and you will be able to work with extension! You can use this id to sync data between your computers - just put your id into ID field and click "Save".
